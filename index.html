Waste

The Problem

WE MAKE a LOT of WASTE

We are producing gigantic amounts of waste. Total waste in the world is enormous.

2.12 Billion Tons of waste globally each year

Some of it is recycled but a lot is simply dumped, causing problems for people and the environment.

Dangerous SHW (solid household waste)!

To dangerous solid waste are:

  • Batteries and accumulators
  • electrical appliances
  • lacquers
  • paints and cosmetics
  • fertilizers and pesticides
  • bit chemistry
  • medical waste
  • mercury-containing thermometers
  • barometers
  • pressure monitors
  • lamps

This type of waste pose a danger to the environment if they get through sewage into sewage or be washed out of the landfill and ground / surface water.

Batteries and mercury-containing instruments will be are safe until their body is damaged, then mercury, alkali, lead and zinc will become elements of air pollution, underground and surface water.
